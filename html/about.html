Red Tails Origin

Established in 2022. Red Tail Craft is a locally owned and operated construction company centered around a passion for building in a manner that seamlessly blends attention to detail, form, and function. We pride ourselves on a quality product and experience for our clients as well as an environment encouraging teaching and learning for our team.

My Story

The Skilled Craftsmen Shaping Your Vision at Red Tail Craft Construction

Garrett Kane

CEO

I started Red Tail Craft in 2021 after nearly a decade in the custom residential construction industry. I've been fortunate to have learned the craft from highly skilled and knowledgeable teams that allowed me to go from a laborer, to carpenter, to foreman. I love building, teaching, collaborating, and I strive to continue to learn and improve daily. My experiences learning from others who are passionate about building made me love what I do, take pride in how I do it, and gave me a desire to pass that on to a team and our clients.
